Common questions

Does SuperPlayer include channels?

No. SuperPlayer is a player for your own Stalker, STB / MAC address, or IPTV M3U provider. It does not sell, host, or bundle TV channels, playlists, or media content.

Why will a provider not load?

Check that the provider type, portal URL, username, password, and MAC address are correct. Some providers block unknown devices or expired accounts, so you may need to contact your IPTV provider.

How do I manage Pro?

Use Settings in the app to upgrade or restore purchases. App Store subscriptions are managed through your Apple Account subscription settings.
