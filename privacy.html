Privacy Policy

Effective July 2026

SuperPlayer IPTV Stalker STB ("the app") is built and operated by Alberto Montagnese. The app is a player for connecting to your own IPTV/Stalker portal provider. The app does not provide, sell, host, or bundle TV channels, playlists, or media content.

Data you enter

You may enter a portal URL, username, password, nickname, and device ID so the app can connect to your IPTV provider. This information is stored locally on your device and is used only to connect to the provider you choose. We do not operate your IPTV provider and we do not receive your portal credentials.

Usage analytics

The app may collect limited usage analytics to help us understand whether the app opens successfully, whether portal connection and playback flows work, and which product areas need improvement. Analytics events may include app version, platform, an anonymous install identifier, session identifier, broad event names such as app open, portal connection success or failure, playback start or error, favorite changes, region filter changes, and search query length buckets.

Analytics events may include the provider or playlist URL you entered so we can understand provider compatibility and diagnose failed portal or playlist loads. Before sending this value, the app redacts URL username/password fields and common password query parameters such as password, pass, pwd, and passwd. Analytics events do not include your saved portal password, channel names, search text, viewing history, or the media you watch.

Diagnostics

Apple may provide us with aggregate crash and diagnostic reports if you have enabled sharing diagnostics with developers in your Apple device settings. These reports are provided by Apple and help us fix bugs.

Data we do not sell

We do not sell or rent your data. We do not use your data for third-party advertising.

Third parties

Your IPTV provider receives the connection information you enter when the app connects to that provider. Apple processes App Store purchases, downloads, crash diagnostics, and App Store analytics under Apple's own terms and privacy policy. If analytics collection is enabled, analytics events are sent to our analytics endpoint for product improvement only.

Your choices

  • You can remove stored portal profiles by deleting them in the app.
  • You can delete the app to remove local app data from your device.
  • You can control Apple diagnostics sharing in your device privacy settings.

Children

The app is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

If this policy materially changes, the updated version will be published here with a new effective date.
